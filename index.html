What We Provide to Students

Students of all kinds can enroll!.

Business

We empower students with the Knowledge of the economy by seriously teaching the bussiness disciplines that is Commerce and Entrepreneurship.

Academic & Performance

Excellent academic performance is a must at Granite Bay High School since all the students are taught using the latest books according the latest curriculum!.

Sports

Students refresh their minds with different kinds of sports forexample football, netball, Rugyby and so many more.

Science & Technology

Granite Bay High School is having a full and well equipped science laboratory. It comprises of all the neccessities for chemistry, Biology, Physics and Agriculture Praticals. For science Teachers, all teach latest topics .

Art & Media

For Art Students, we have a separate lab to help them get inspired and increase their creativity levels.The lab is always open!!.

Communication Skills

At Granite Bay High School, we emphasize English speaking even when students are out of the school premises. We always hold different debates in order to level up students' confidence.

Language

All the communication is done in English at Granite Bay High School.In addition to that, other languages like Literature, German and French are employed to help discover more of the languages.

ICT

The technology part is well eveidenced by the presence of the separate computer lab equipped with latest and updated. The school has a free wifi connection for Teachers to help them make extensive researches.
